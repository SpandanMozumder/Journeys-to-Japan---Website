Hi!

My name is Spandan Mozumder and first of all thank you for visiting my website. Although I have not gone to Japan, but it is the country that I would like to visit when I will start travelling in near future.

I am a college student pursuing Bachelors in the Department of Computer Science, who is also a great travel enthusiast and if you are thinking why is this guy, who has never travelled in life making a webpage about travel; it's just that in order for us to get jobs in the IT Sector, we need to do projects in the respective fields in order to appeal to the employer that I have capability to work in a professional environent and contribute to professional projects. So while deciding what to do I thought that maybe do something that will be both productive to put in my resume and also engaging enough to make me personally interested in this, and as a result this idea of creating a travel webpage came. But then I thought that if I am going to make a blog, I have to put my travel experiences in it, but I haven't gone anywhere, so why not make a website about the information about the country which i want to visit the most.

If you are asking why Japan; here is an obvious answer. For those who have researched about this country, they would understand it much better that Japan as a country itself is beautiful. The beauty is breathtaking, even in metropolitan cities. The people there are the best in the whole wide world as you would never find some more hospitable and respectful as the Japanese. The country's flourishment is mainly because of its people, and their culture. There is rarely and vulgarity seen amongst the people. The countryside is beautiful, the city life is engaging, the food is delicious and vibrant and the culture is unique and vibrant. If words didn't convinve you, go open youtube and see it for yourself, why you should visit this country at least twice in your lifetime.
